About our project

The ultimate poker bluff machine. A bluff in poker is when you make a bet in an attempt to make your opponents fold a hand that's possibly better than yours, helping you win the pot. Knowing when to bluff calls upon a specific bluffing skillset. Some of the factors that one should consider when bluffing include player types, position, chip stacks, etc. Our machine learning model takes these things into consideration and predicts how likely you are to win the game. Should you fold, or should you go for the win?
